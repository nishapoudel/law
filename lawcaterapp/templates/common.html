About Lawcater

Life is synonymous to stories. And stories belong to the ordinary people just as much as they are highlighted to be about the ones in the limelight. Our objective as 'Lawcaters' is to bring out the unheard and underrepresented stories of newness and timeless truths, of ecstasies and pains, of pioneering and stereotypes, to a platform visible to more common sharers of such experiences.
